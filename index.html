• Americans spend an average of 1,597 hours per year watching television.

    But the way they consume television has changed drastically in recent years.

  • Since 2009, online television consumption has increased from 44 million users to 95 million in 2014, with a projcted 99.2 million in 2015. Meanwhile, viewers of primetime television has decreased.

  • Netflix and Hulu are the primary sources of paid online television consumption. However, other sources are quickly gaining popularity like Crackle and Amazon Prime for example.

  • While, online streaming service usage has increased in recent years, their revenue is still pretty far behind the other major media groups. This is likely due to their high volume of assets.
